About us

Who we are and what we do We are a community organisation made up of Ukrainian & British nationals working from our base in Milton Keynes and our mission is to help the people in Ukraine survive this terrible conflict by delivering aid directly into Ukraine. We collect donated aid in the form of food, hygiene items, general medicines, medical/surgical goods and any other humanitarian aid items for shipping and free distribution by our "trusted partners" in Ukraine. Raising funds for the purchase of humanitarian aid and to pay for the transportation of aid into Ukraine, as well as for purchase of any other non-lethal aid that may be requested.

We need support

We are constantly looking for partners, so we appeal to business owners and all caring people who want help. We need:

  • hygiene products, clothes, shoes;
  • baby food, diapers, toys;
  • any food (dairy products, vegetables, fruits, cereals);
  • medicines, tactical medicine and medical equipment;
  • drones, night vision devices, thermal imagers;
  • equipment (body armor, helmets, military shoes, balaclavas, buffs, knee/elbow pads, boots, gloves);
  • thermal and ordinary underwear;
  • sleeping bags, blankets, mattresses;
  • power banks, lanterns, batteries;
  • generators, shovels;
